Chicken and Dumpling Casserole

This chicken and dumpling casserole is pleasant to the mouth

A next time will surely come after eating the chicken and dumpling casserole

Ingredient

  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breast, cut into small pieces
  • 1 cup sliced carrot
  • 1/2 cup sliced celery
  • 1 cube chicken bouilion. or more to taste
  • 1/3 cup better
  • 1/2 cup of chopped onion
  • 1/2 teaspoon poultry seasoning
  • 2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cup of chicken broth
  • 1 (16.3 ounce) package refrigerated biscuit dough, separated
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Steps

  1. Preheat the to 175 degrees C. Grease a large baking dish
  2. Combine chicken, carrot, celery and boullion in a large pot and add enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, cover and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the centers, 10 to 15 minutes. Drain, reserving chicken and broth in another pot
  3. Melt butter in the same pot over medium heat. Cook onion and garlic in the hot butter for 5 minutes. stir in flour, pultry seasoning, basil, thyme and pepper. Stir in chicken broth and milk slowly. Add chicken mixture and peas, simmer over medium-low heat until thick, 10 to 15 minntes. pour mixture into the prepared baking dish
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until mixture is bubling , 20 to 25 minutes. Remove from the oven and place biscuit on top. cover and bake until biscuit are golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes more
